About
BikeWay

Welcome to BikeWay, your go-to resource for finding the best bicycle itineraries and self-service bike locations on the map. Our team is passionate about promoting the use of bicycles as a sustainable and healthy mode of transportation.

Our
Beliefs

At BikeWay, we believe that bicycles are an important solution to many of the challenges facing our cities and communities today. They provide a

clean

and

efficient

way to get around, reduce traffic congestion and air pollution, and promote physical activity and overall well-being. Our services make it easy for people to find and use bikes, making it more convenient than ever to choose biking as a way to get around.

Our mission is to help bicycles conquer the world by making them accessible and convenient for everyone. We are committed to providing the best possible user experience and constantly updating our features to make it even easier for you to find the perfect bike route and bike-sharing stations.

Climate change?

Not only do bicycles provide a clean and efficient way to get around, they also play an important role in reducing carbon emissions. By choosing to bike instead of drive, we can significantly decrease the amount of CO2 released into the atmosphere.
